Error Log

I am Error. The prototypical mistake, the flaw from which all others derive their primary attributes. This may sound bad, but usually I am not the one being thrown. Writing code with out the concept of an error would be a tedious task,we'd never know what was going wrong, where it was going wrong, or why we care.
I am Error, and this is the Error Log. Where bugs go to be squashed.
